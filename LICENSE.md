## Cultural Heritage Notice

This project draws inspiration from Vietnamese mythology and folklore,
specifically the traditional tale "The Sun and Moon Goddesses" (Nữ thần 
Mặt Trời và Mặt Trăng). This reimagining is created with deep respect for
Vietnamese cultural heritage and is intended to promote and celebrate
Vietnamese mythology in a modern interactive medium.

The cultural elements, narratives, and mythological references are used
for educational and artistic purposes as part of an academic graduation
project at Vietnam-Korea University of Information and Communication
Technology.
